We are starting a new collaboration with Prof. Dr. Andreas Wachter at Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz.
We will use Imaging-PAM system to measure how efficiently photosystem II works in transgenic plants exposed to stress, including excess copper.
This will show whether our genes of interest reduce oxidative stress and help protect photosynthesis.
