We are excited to begin a new collaboration with Prof. Dr. Andreas Wachter at Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz.

We will use Imaging-PAM to measure how efficiently photosystem II works in transgenic plants exposed to abiotic stress, including excess copper. This will help us test whether our genes of interest reduce oxidative stress and protect photosynthesis.

We look forward to seeing what these experiments reveal.
